Answers for Collectors

Purchasing Guidelines

Are your timepieces authentic?

+

Yes, all Stackly watches sold directly through our online boutique or authorized showrooms come with a certificate of authenticity, an embossed metallic guarantee card, and a laser-etched serial number matching our registry records.

How works shipping and insurance?

+

We provide complimentary secure shipping worldwide via armored courier service (DHL/FedEx Priority). All shipments are fully insured for their retail value during transit. Packages require an adult signature upon delivery.

What does the 25-year warranty cover?

+

Our 25-year warranty covers all internal mechanism defects, balance alignment issues, and hand synchronization. It does not cover wear and tear of straps, battery life (for quartz servicing), or external scratching of the sapphire crystal.
