Crafted Journey

The Servicing Journey

01

Diagnostics

We test dial rate, balance amplitude, and magnetic friction to identify structural errors.

02

Disassembly

The case is separated and movement is stripped down to individual gears, jewels, and bridges.

03

Calibrating

Parts are cleaned, lubricated, and hand-adjusted for timing drift in multiple gravity positions.

04

Gaskets

All seal gaskets are replaced, and crowns are tested in pressure chamber up to 10 ATM.

05

Verification

A rigorous 48-hour automated test runs to confirm sweep consistency before final shipping.
