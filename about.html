Our Heritage

For over a century, Stackly has defined the boundaries of mechanical art, building timepieces that transcend fashion and celebrate legacy.

A Century of Precision

Milestones in Time

1920

The First Caliber

Master watchmaker Giovanni Vianelli opens the first Stackly workshop in Geneva, creating pocket watches for European navigators.

1948

Pioneering Waterproofing

We introduce our first hermetically sealed case, setting new standards for maritime exploration and professional timing.

1972

The Chrono-Revolution

Release of the first double-register chronograph movement, achieving unprecedented time-fraction measurement for aviators.

2005

Silicon Technology

Integrating silicon escapement wheels to counter magnetic fields, resulting in near-perfect mechanical stability.

2026

A Sustainable Future

Transitioning all production to run on 100% renewable energy and introducing vegetable-tanned, conflict-free leather straps.
